Crystal Counter Game

Instructions

There are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will be shown a random number at the start of the game.

Win the game by matching your total score to random number. Lose the game if your total score exceeds random number shown.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of crystals
